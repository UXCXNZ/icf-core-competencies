## Using the Marker Maps

Coaches can use these maps to:

1. **Plan sessions**: Identify which competency clusters to focus on at different phases
2. **Self-assess**: Evaluate which competency relationships they execute well vs. need development
3. **Develop skills**: Focus on mastering related competencies together rather than in isolation
4. **Maintain flow**: Understand how competencies naturally transition during coaching conversations
5. **Troubleshoot**: When sessions feel stuck, identify which competency clusters might help

## Creating Custom Marker Maps

Coaches are encouraged to create their own personalized marker maps that reflect:

1. Their unique coaching style and strengths
2. The specific needs of different client populations
3. How competencies manifest in different coaching contexts (executive, life, team, etc.)
4. Their developmental journey and current focus areas

A custom map can be created by:
1. Identifying your 3-5 strongest competencies
2. Mapping which other competencies naturally flow from these strengths
3. Identifying competencies that feel challenging or disconnected
4. Creating deliberate bridges between your strengths and development areas
